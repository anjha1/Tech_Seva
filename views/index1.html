    How TechSeva Works

    📞

    Step 1: Book Service

    Easily book a service request through our intuitive app, website, or via a simple call to our IVR system. Select your appliance type and describe the issue.

    🛠️

    Step 2: Technician Auto-Assignment

    Our AI-powered system intelligently matches your request to the best-suited local technician available in your area. You'll receive instant notification of assignment.

    💵

    Step 3: Repair & Secure Payment

    The technician performs the repair. Review the auto-generated quotation, pay securely via UPI/COD, and receive a digital invoice and warranty for peace of mind.
