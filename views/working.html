    Customer Journey: Get Your Appliance Fixed

    1 Book a Service

    Choose from our wide range of services including electrical repairs, plumbing, appliance servicing, and more. You can book through:

    • Mobile App: Available in multiple regional languages
    • IVR System: Call our number and book without a smartphone
    • Website: Simple online booking form

    2 AI Technician Matching

    Our proprietary AI algorithm considers multiple factors to find the best technician for your job:

    • Location: Finds the nearest available technician
    • Skills: Matches based on your specific appliance/problem
    • Ratings: Prioritizes highly-rated technicians
    • Availability: Finds someone who can come quickly

    3 Diagnosis & Quotation

    When the technician arrives, they follow our standardized diagnostic process:

    • Take photos of the appliance/problem
    • Select faulty parts from our digital inventory
    • System automatically generates a detailed quote including:
      • Parts needed (with brand options)
      • Labor charges (based on job complexity)
      • Travel fee (distance-based)

    4 Approval & Payment

    Before any work begins, you'll receive the quote for approval:

    • View the quote on your app or via SMS
    • See exactly what parts will be used and their costs
    • Choose to approve or reject the quote
    • Secure payment options:
      • UPI (PhonePe, Google Pay, etc.)
      • Credit/Debit Cards
      • Cash (with digital receipt)

    5 Repair & Warranty

    Once approved, the technician completes the repair with full transparency:

    • Photos of installed parts
    • Digital invoice with warranty details
    • Follow-up support if needed
    • Standard 30-day warranty on all repairs
